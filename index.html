Climbing & Paragliding

Includes: Dedicated Site Guide | Accommodation


CLIMBING : Our climbing initiatives offer an unparalleled adventure at Asubone and Krobo, presenting diverse opportunities for both intensive bouldering and exhilarating sport climbing across Ghana's stunning landscapes. We've ensured a comprehensive setup, providing essential gear like crashpads and climbing shoes, along with expert local guides on-site to assist with navigation and technique, guaranteeing that climbers of all levels can immerse themselves fully in the experience; simply plan your visit, and we'll handle all necessary arrangements for an unforgettable ascent.

For certified solo pilots, we warmly invite you to bring your gear and join our vibrant community to fly our incredible thermals, promising an exceptional and challenging aerial adventure atop one of Ghana's most iconic peaks.
