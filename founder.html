Kwame Owusu
Founder & Lead Tour Guide

Growing up in the heart of Kwahu, I was surrounded by breathtaking landscapes, vibrant traditions, and a community that welcomed every traveler as family. My passion for adventure and storytelling led me to create Let's Tour Kwahu, a brand dedicated to sharing the hidden gems and authentic experiences of our region with the world.

I believe travel is more than sightseeing; it's about connecting with people, culture, and nature. Through our tours, I aim to empower local communities, preserve our heritage, and inspire visitors to see Ghana through the eyes of its people. Every journey with us is a chance to discover, learn, and belong.

My vision for LTK Tours is to serve the people of Kwahu and all travelers with tours and travel services rooted in quality customer care and value addition. Beyond being the founder, I am deeply committed to social service and community impact. Since 2019, I have served as a volunteer director for the MentorHer Ghana girls’ empowerment program, established five in-school libraries, and championed sanitation awareness initiatives by providing bins across communities. Through my Neaonnim Volunteer Program, I continue to create opportunities that connect volunteers with the Kwahu community, ensuring that travel with LTK Tours is not just about adventure, but also about leaving a positive mark.

Thank you for joining our story. I look forward to welcoming you to Kwahu and sharing the adventure!
